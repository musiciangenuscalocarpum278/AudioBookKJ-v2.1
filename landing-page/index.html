Audio Studio Workflow

The hub for scripting, voice casting, and rendering lifelike audio.

02

Video Studio Workflow

Your AI Director's chair. Create visual assets, construct scene graphs, and generate stunning visuals via FlowKit/Veo.

  • Visual Asset Management: Maintain character consistency by marking reference images.
  • Node-Based Scene Graph: Visually break down dialogue into interconnected establishing shots and B-rolls.
  • Director Notes & Keyframing: Inject exact user intents to generate anchor frames before rendering the full video.
03

Post-Production & Export

The final stage. Mix multi-track audio and video timelines like a professional DAW, then export the final product.

  • Synchronized Timelines: Effortlessly adjust start times, trim, and balance volume across overlapping tracks.
  • Adaptive Preview Monitor: Real-time playback supporting both 16:9 Landscape and 9:16 Portrait orientations.
  • Seamless Export: Backend FFmpeg handles the heavy lifting, smartly scaling and padding visuals for the perfect MP3 or MP4 export.
